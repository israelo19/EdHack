How do you find circle Measurements?






To find circle measurements, you have to know two terms, radius, diameter and circumference. A radius is half of a circle, this line is drawn diagonally across the circle. A diameter is the full diagonal distance, so it,s the Radius times 2. To find the circumference, do C = 2(pi)(r). If the circumfrence of sliced orange is 20 centimeters, what is the radius and diameter? To find radius with only the circumference given do this, r = c/(2)(pi), to find the diameter, d = 2(r).
