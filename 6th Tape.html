Equations in Tape Diagrams

How do you solve an Equation in a tape diagram?
Lets take a look at the first Tape diagram which we know that the total value is 12. To find X we must first put it in a equation. The Equation would look like this X + 4 = 12, then remove 4 from both sides X + 4 - 4 = 12 - 4 and simplify to x = 8 which is our final answer. Lets take a look at the secound Tape diagram. Lets make it into an equation 4x = 12. First step is devide both side by 4 4x / 4 = 12 / 4, to simplify to x = 3 our final answer.
