Area

So how do we get the area?
Here is an example of an area problem. You can break this shape up into two shapes a rectangle on the top a rectangle on the bottom. From the numbers you can see the height is 3 and the width is 4. So to calculate the area you multiply the 4 and the 3 to get 12. Now to find the area of the second rectangle. We can tell by the numbers the height is 2 and the width is 6. So to get the Area of the secound rectangle you must multiply 2 by 6 to get 12. Now the final step is to add the two rectangles together which is 12 plus 12 to get the area of 24.
