Introducing Proportional Relationships

How do you find a Proportional Relationship?






When looking for proportional relationships, you deal with independent and dependent variables. On the first image, a company ordered cases of paper towels, the independent variable, and in those paper towels, there are rolls, the dependent variable. For every 1 case of paper towels, there are 12 rolls in it. Knowing this, you can determine x number of cases will get y number of rolls, by either multiplying by 12 or dividing by 12.
