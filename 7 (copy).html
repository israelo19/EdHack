How do you find a Scale Drawing?






Real places and objects represented in 2D are scale drawings. A scale drawing tells us how actual measurements are represented on the drawing. The first scale drawing on the left shows some of the tallest buildings modeled. It is an image representation, in 2 dimention, of The Great Pyramid, The Eiffel Tower, The Willis Tower, CN Tower, and Burj Khalifa. The second image shows a scale drawing of a school bus, a quarter, and the subway lines of Boston Massachusetts. Here you can see how all those three items are in 2 dimentions and are the scales of the real objects.
