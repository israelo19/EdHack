Angles, Triangles, and Prisms

How to determine missing angles?






When looking for missing angles, remember these key words. Conguency - when two angles are opposite from each other, they are the same. Corresponding angles - when two or more angles equal 90°. Supplementary angles - when two or more angles equal 180°. Match these equations to their image:
1. g + h = 180
2. g = h
3. 2h + g = 90
4. g + h + 48 = 180
5. g + h + 35 = 180
